STANDARD BENEFITS PACKAGE

All full-time employees are eligible for the following benefits:

HEALTH & WELLNESS:
- Medical insurance (employee + dependents)
- Dental and vision coverage
- Mental health support program
- Annual wellness stipend: 500 USD

TIME OFF:
- 20-25 days paid vacation (based on seniority)
- Public holidays (country-specific)
- 5 sick days per year
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT:
- Annual learning budget: 2,000 - 4,000 USD
- Conference attendance (1-2 per year)
- Internal mentorship program
- Access to online learning platforms

WORK FLEXIBILITY:
- Hybrid work model (2-3 days in office)
- Flexible working hours (core hours: 10 AM - 4 PM)
- Home office equipment allowance (one-time: 1,000 USD)

FINANCIAL:
- Competitive base salary (reviewed annually)
- Performance-based bonuses
- Stock options (for senior roles)

PERKS:
- Team lunches and social events
- Modern office with ergonomic setup
- Free snacks and beverages
- Annual team retreat
